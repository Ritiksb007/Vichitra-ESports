FAQ

General Questions

Who can participate in your tournaments? +

Our tournaments are open to gamers of all skill levels. Some events may have specific eligibility criteria, which will be mentioned in the event details.

How do I register for a tournament? +

You can register by visiting the Tournaments page, selecting the event, and completing the registration form.

What games do you host tournaments for? +

We host tournaments for a wide range of games, including popular titles like League of Legends, Valorant, Call of Duty, Fortnite, and more.

Can I collaborate with your company as a streamer or content creator? +

We’re always looking for talented creators! Reach out through our Partnerships page to discuss collaboration opportunities.

How can I partner with your company? +

We’re open to partnerships with teams, organizations, and brands. Contact us at officialvichitraesports@gmail.com to explore collaboration opportunities.

Vichitra Esports is a leading organization dedicated to transforming the Indian esports landscape. We aim to empower gaming talent, foster inclusivity, and create platforms for players to showcase their skills. Alongside flagship events like the BGMI LAN Event – Nagpur 2024, we also organize a variety of online gaming tournaments across multiple genres, building a thriving community that connects grassroots talent to national opportunities while celebrating the dynamic spirit of esports.
