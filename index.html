About The Event

BGMI LAN Event – Nagpur 2024 is a premier Maharashtra-Level ESports tournament organized by Vichitra Esports, aimed at showcasing the finest gaming talent while fostering inclusivity and community engagement. This event promises to be a milestone in the Indian esports landscape, bringing together players, enthusiasts, and stakeholders from across the nation.

Event Highlights

Prize Pool: A celebration of skill and excellence, rewarding top performances.
Inclusivity Focus: Free entry to encourage widespread participation and diversity.
Grassroots Development: A platform for underdog teams to make their mark in the competitive scene.
Collaboration with 12+ Colleges: Strengthening connections between local talent and national opportunities.
Esports Ecosystem: A platform for aspiring players to rise and shine.
Pan-Maharashtra Participation: Open to teams and players from all over the Maharastra, showcasing Maharashtra’s vibrant gaming talent.

Our Vision

To create an unforgettable experience that not only celebrates gaming but also strengthens the esports ecosystem by providing a platform for aspiring players to rise and shine.

Whether you're a seasoned professional or a rising star, BGMI LAN Event – Nagpur 2024 offers an unparalleled opportunity to compete, connect, and celebrate the spirit of esports. Join us for this landmark event and be a part of India’s gaming revolution!
